Hi, I'm Arina Ghaheri A Product Designer

I'm a final-year student at the University of Tehran pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Industrial and Product Design with a minor in Computer Science. My work sits at the intersection of design and technology, where I explore how human-centered design principles and computational thinking can create more intuitive and accessible digital experiences. Currently, I'm focused on Human-Computer Interaction research, particularly in areas of AI-based interface design, user experience methodologies, and novel interaction technologies. Through my academic work and professional experience as a UX/Product Designer, I'm passionate about designing systems that empower users and enhance their interaction with technology.
